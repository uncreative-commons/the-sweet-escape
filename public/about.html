THE SWEET ESCAPE

About

The Sweet Escape was the Hellenic Game Jam 2013 entry by UNCREATIVE COMMONS.

Through the player's intervention, a piece of candy about to be wrapped, unwrapped and sucked upon may or may not be saved from certain death. But freedom doesn't come for free and sometimes it takes a little team effort.

This sweet post-modern massive-multiplayer online puzzle platformer has been conjured up in the minds of Raz, Flow, Spyro and wizgrav.
